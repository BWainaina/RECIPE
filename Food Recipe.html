PILAU

Ingredients

  1. 1 Large Onion
    2.
  2. 2-3 Garlic cloves crushed
    3.
  3. A little ginger crushed
    4.
  4. 2-3 tablespoons ground Pilau Masala
    5.
  5. 1/2 Kg cubed beef
    6.
  6. 4 Tomatoes
    7.
  7. 2 cups of rice
    8.
  8. 3 cups Hot water
    9.
  9. Salt to taste
    10.
  10. 2-2.5 tablespoons cooking oil

Method
  1. Heat oil in a sufuria, add in the onion and ginger
    2.
  2. Let this cook over a medium flame until browned
    3.
  3. Stir in the meat and replace lid over sufuria
    4.
  4. Let the meat cook for till tender
    5.
  5. Add pilau masala and salt while stirring continuously to ensure the spices are well-distributed
    6.
  6. Add tomatoes and continue to stir
    7.
  7. Add some water and bring this to boil after covering the sufuria
    8.
  8. Add the washed rice and mix well
  9. Proceed to add 3 cups of water, cover and let this boil over a medium flame for a while
    10.
  10. Reduce the heat and allow the rice to simmer while covered
    11.
  11. After about 5 minutes of cooking, give the rice a stir using a fork and cover
    12.
  12. Repeat the stirring one or two more times to ensure the rice is coking evenly and does not stick to bottom of sufuria
    13.
  13. The rice should cook until all excess water has evaporated
    14.
  14. Once ready, the dish may be accompanied with some kachumbari
